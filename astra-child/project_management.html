Pillars of Client & Asset Protection

1. Licensed Residential Builder & 2-5-10 National Home Warranty

We are a fully licensed and registered residential builder under the BC Housing licensing system (License #52603). To ensure complete peace of mind, every new construction project is fully backed by the industry-standard **2-5-10 Year National Home Warranty Protection Program** (covering 2 years on materials and labor, 5 years on the building envelope, and 10 years on structural components). This represents the ultimate shield for your luxury residential investment.

2. Civil Contracting & Utility Site Preparation

We specialize in regional site preparation, steep-slope excavation, foundation drainage, and utility trenching. Our team is actively completing the final registration gates for the official **BC Hydro Registered Civil Contractor List** to legally execute municipal utility connections, and we operate in strict compliance with **WorkSafeBC 30M33 electrical distribution safety standards**.

3. BC Bill 44 Multiplex Zoning Feasibility

Navigating British Columbia's new **Bill 44 zoning legislation** (allowing up to 3 to 4 multi-family units on standard single-family lots) requires precise code analysis. We coordinate with local municipal planning offices in Squamish and the RMOW in Whistler to determine set-backs, height envelopes, and utility connection requirements, ensuring your property's development yield is legally maximized.
